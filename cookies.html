Our cookie policy

HANDS IN THE COOKIE JAR

This website abides by EU GDPR law when dealing with cookies.

We understand that new regulation can be scary. We saw all the media attention on leaked data and companies selling off your personal data for profit.

Here at BlackSkull we are hard working folk just like you. We would be upset if someone sold our data so we make sure your data never leaves our database.

SAFETY FIRST

What is our cookie policy?

Well put simply this website saves one cookie, and it does not save any personal data.

What exactly are cookies?

Cookies are small snippets of data that a website collects. Learn more about cookies.

Do we store cookies?

Yes we do. We save two cookies.

Which cookie do we store?

We use something called Google Analytics. This is a service run by Google that allows us to check traffic, where visitors are coming from, how long they stay on the site, what their conversion rate is, what countries they are visiting from and a few more similar metrics.

It does not store personal data like names, addresses etc. It simply logs where the visitor came from and what they did on the website. Google analytics is used to improve website performance.

Learn more about Google Analytics.

We also use a service called Hotjar. Hotjar provides anonymous visitor metrics to us. It lets us know what elements on each page users click on and provides us with heatmaps so we can make improvements to our design. It does not store IP addresses or personal data.

Can I block cookies on this website?

Of course! We recommend this guide to help you disable cookies in your browser.

If you have any more questions about our usage of cookies please feel free to call or email us.
