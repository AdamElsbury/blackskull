OUR SERVICES

We take pride in the fact that we can provide pretty much anything an SME needs for the web.

Companies do things in different ways. Some people believe the only way to set up a successful business is to specialise in a market niche and become the best at it. This is true for many markets but for the web, and our specific target market, its a little more tricky.

We like to think of it this way. If you're coming to us for a solution, you either don't have the time or expertise to create one yourself. Or both. So you probably don't know much about the industry or have the time to research all the different services.

We know this because we did the research! So from strategy through to deployment we can handle everything. You pay one price for one solution. Nice and simple!

FIGURING OUT HOW YOUR USERS TICK

Research

A good research strategy is the foundation for any design studio worth their salt. Here we can establish who your users are, what their goals & needs are and create a website to address those goals.

We also do competitor research to establish benchmarks and see what other websites do well and where they are lacking.

A good research strategy is like revising for a science exam with Stephen Hawking.

Its kinda cheating, really. Think logically. If we have the know how to create good, engaging content to question and understand your user base, establish what their goals are then its easy to work backwards from that point to create a website!

All our designs are data driven and specifically crafted to meet your user goals. No guesswork. All we do is ask the right questions and know how to translate them into actionable tasks.

BENEFITS OF A GOOD RESEARCH STRATEGY

  • Avoid wrong decisions early to avoid expensive fixes later
  • Understanding users with tangible data to easily identify success or failiure
  • Create benchmarks for the project to drive work scheduling
  • Knowing who your audience is allows you to create products they want
  • Create accurate project scope to avoid going over budget or wasting time

DESIGNING A SOLUTION THAT ADDRESSES USER PROBLEMS

Design

First we create a User Experience design to understand your user journey from landing on your site to landing a conversion and creating all the functionality and content they require along the way.

Once we have the usability design we add bespoke graphics, icons, logos and colour schemes to represent your brand online.

Good design avoids user frustration and guides your user through an easy to use, visually appealing interface.

Imagine a shopping centre with no signage or maps, and shops were not categorised and hidden in hard to reach areas. And you're trying to find a store. Frustrating right? That's exactly what it feels like to a user who is trying to achieve a goal on a poorly designed site.

Now imagine a shopping centre with great maps and easy to find stores but there's trash, broken windows and rats running everywhere! That's what its like when a website is lacking visually.

Fear no more! We can cater to both. Theres no point having an easy to use website if it looks awful. We will create great usability combined with appealing visuals to craft a balanced experience.

BENEFITS OF GOOD DESIGN

  • Good UX design leads to good conversion rates improving sales
  • Reduces user frustration on the website
  • Reduces production length & cost by testing prototypes
  • Allows for good brand representation online
  • Avoids unnecessary content & reduces number of "steps" the user has to take to achieve a goal
  • Creates customised solution for YOUR users that templates cannot achieve
  • Usability testing provides tangible evidence of problems in the design & allows us to fix them easily
