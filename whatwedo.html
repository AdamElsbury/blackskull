What we do

WHAT WE DO

Our forte is researching your users and creating an effective design based solution to cater to their specific needs.

We are a design focused Web design & Development studio based out of South Wales. All our members are degree educated in Web design & development. We use a professional, industry standard work flow. We love working with SME's & individuals who bring fresh ideas to the UK marketplace.

We work with an "Ego free" attitude. None of our ideas or solutions are free from scrutiny - whether internal or external. We are constantly striving to improve and deliver the best solutions possible.

All of our work is crafted with the user experience in mind.

Design

In the design phase of your project we will conduct user & market research to accurately create a solution that represents and addresses your individual user needs.

We then design the site structure, content strategy, navigation and UX. Once that is done we add on the graphic design to give your site its own personal touch & branding.

Build

In the build phase of your project we take the designs we have created and turn it into a live product. All the nerdy bits. Coding, unit tests, responsive testing, browser compatibility.

We probably won't update you much during this point unless you express an interest. Coding is boring to talk about.
