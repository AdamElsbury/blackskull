What we do

GOOD USER EXPERIENCE MAKES THE WEB A BETTER PLACE

We plan, design & build blazing fast, easy to use websites with gorgeous bespoke branding.

We are a design focused Web design & Development studio based out of South Wales. We specialise in Web Design, Web Development, eCommerce & bespoke branding.

With over 4 years experience, BlackSkull is an enthusiastic studio offering creative, user focused solutions to solve problems experienced by SME's and professional individuals when it comes to their online presence.

At the core of our working process is our user experience methodology. All of our projects start with a user problem(s). We then craft a creative solution to solve those problems and ensure your websites provide the best user experience possible.

HOW WE DO IT

Our process is entirely intended on achieving your brand goals by focusing on the user first.

Sounds simple but so many companies these days neglect the user focused design process. Everything we do starts first and foremost with understanding how your users tick, what their goals are and then laying out a solution carefully crafted to achieve them.

You generate revenue through your users. We craft solutions specifically for them.

Our process

UNDERSTANDING

Understanding your product & brand strategy

What's the point in creating any bespoke web design if we don't fully understand the product or service you are trying to sell?

We take the time to get to know your brand, products & goals. Once we understand where you need to be its easy to craft the steps for how to get there.

USER RESARCH

Identifying your user goals

In line with our user first design strategy we peruse through any data you might have or go to external sources to fully understand who your users are, what they want and what they don't like.

JOURNEY

Crafting your user journey

Using our swanky new user data we can construct a user journey to establish how to funnel users to your site, give them the necessary steps they require to reach the conversion point and providing a means with which to convert them.

USER EXPERIENCE

Translating the user journey into tangible web elements

Splitting the process up into digestible chunks we can plan what content & functionality your users require on your website.

We follow the user journey adding content and elements as we go to formulate an easy to use interface.

A SIGHT FOR SORE EYES

Adding the visual design

Once we have all the user experience finished its time to add custom graphics, transitions, borders, backgrounds and branding to give your website the visual pop to attract new visitors.

GEEKING OUT

Translating the design into reality with code

Now its time to build the damn thing! Here we work through the design page by page coding until we have the finished product.

Not much to really say here other than we ensure the product loads quickly, all our code is backed up and your site will rank highly in Google. Once the site passes our internal testing its time to go live!

THE MONEY MAKER

Digital marketing to increase traffic & make money

There is no point in having a great website if no one sees it. Here we will advise which channels & demographics to advertise in.

We do this with keyword and market research and give you a digital marketing strategy to ensure your new site attracts visitors and generates revenue.
