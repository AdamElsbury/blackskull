OUR PRICING STRATEGY

Nothing is more annoying than hidden charges or paying for services you don't need. Introducing our Modular Pricing.

Ooh sounds fancy! Well it kinda is. BlackSkull believes in providing a service over trying to squeeze every penny out of a client.

We don't charge by the hour. We charge based on what modules you require. Lets say you own a small dog grooming service and you need a simple 3 page website to promote your prices, location and contact details. You probably don't need a full usability test or digital marketing strategy. So you only pay for what you need.

On the flip side if you have an adventurous new start up idea and want us to give you the full treatment, then that's okay too. We will advise which modules you need but ultimately the decision is up to you. Budget dependant of course. We don't work for free ;)
