ITS QUESTION TIME!

Frequently asked questions

Everyone has questions or concerns when entering into a new industry. Web design can be confusing with all the buzzwords and ambiguous quotes. Let us help.

Q.

Why do I need a professionally designed website?

A.

This Marketo survey shows that the highest proportion of a companies marketing budget is invested in website optimisation followed by social media and SEO. Why? Because the marketing industry has moved from aggressive advertising to lead generation.

It is both cheaper and provides a better ROI to have a fully bespoke, optimised website and digital marketing strategy and to let customers come to you than to aimlessly push ads into the wrong channels. If companies are spending so much money trying to get traffic to their website, then you need to ensure the website is fit for purpose.

Q.

How much does a custom website cost?

A.

Tough to answer. How much does it cost to build a house? Depends on the requirements, functionality, scale, attention to detail and much more. We price projects from a minimum of £500 anywhere up to tens of thousands of pounds.

Ultimately we price a project based on the expected ROI that you receive. The more money it will make you in the long run, the more we charge.

Q.

But I can build a website on a website builder like Wix for almost nothing. Why are you so expensive?

A.

Experience. We are great at what we do. We went to university to study design principles and how to monetize a website. We are better at it than you.

Our projects are all specifically crafted for your individual requirements and user base. We do this with extensive research and testing. When we hand off a project, there is no question that it will make money. You pay for that security.

Q.

How do I know if my company would benefit from a new website?

A.

Easy, we will run an audit on your current website and advise you honestly. You may already have a perfectly functional website. But you probably don't, otherwise you wouldn't be reading this!

We are happy to run a free audit that looks at traffic, conversion rates, why customers leave your site, why they stay on it. What they like and what they don't like. You even get a small report for free.

Q.

A.

Again, tough to answer. Depends on how complex the solution is. We can churn out a templated, cheap website in a few days. Or we could take a month if you require something completely bespoke and you have a large budget.

Ultimately it comes down to your budget. The larger your budget, the more time we put in. Simple.

Q.

How do I get traffic to my website to make money?

A.

Digital marketing, That's the broad term. Within digital marketing you have different channels. Social media, email, paid ads with google, organic search hits etc.

Q.

What is User Experience design? Why do you always mention user first?

A.

User experience or UX design is the process of starting the design of a product with the user first. First we do research to understand who the users are. Then we understand what their needs & frustrations are. Then we create user personas based on the research in order to allow us to think like the users.

Once we know exactly what the users want and what they expect we can create a user journey to plot out the steps required to generate a sale or generate a lead. A large part of user experience design is usability testing where we take a prototype of website, show it to actual users and get them to complete tasks and observe them to identify problems in the design. It takes out all the guesswork.

Q.

How do payments work?

A.

We charge 50% of the quotation up front and 50% upon completion. On larger projects we may add additional stages where we split up the final 50%.

We take half upfront for our own security. Imagine we spend 3 weeks on a project and then the client changes their mind and doesn't want their new website anymore. We have now lost thousands in potential earnings. If we take half up front and you change your mind, we still get to keep the money.
